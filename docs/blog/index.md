    After discovering projects like abagen and the Allen Brain Atlas, I realized that the future of neuroscience lies in collaboration and transparency. !!! quote "On ENEM & Dreams"
    **"Every question I solve brings me closer to UFABC"**

!!! quote "On Computational Neuroscience"
